Portion Calculator

A tool for calculating the cost of a recipe or portion. Because you cannot buy the exact quantity of ingredients needed for your favorite recipe, it can be difficult to know exactly how much a recipe costs you and even harder to know how much a portion would cost. Using this tool you can begin adding ingredients by entering how much you paid for them, how much you bought, and how much you plan to use, to calculate the cost of your recipe. If needed you can then choose the size of your portion.
